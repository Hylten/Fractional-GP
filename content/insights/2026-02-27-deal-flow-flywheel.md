Deal flow systems can become self-sustaining. The flywheel creates momentum that continues indefinitely. The firms that build flywheels generate permanent advantages.

## The Flywheel Components

The deal flow flywheel has interconnected components.

> Sourcing generates relationships.
> Relationships generate deals.
> Deals generate reputation.
> Reputation generates more relationships.
> More relationships generate more sourcing.

The components connect and reinforce.

## The Acceleration Mechanics

The flywheel accelerates over time.

* More deals create more reference points.
* More references create more trust.
* More trust creates more opportunities.
* More opportunities create more deals.

Each rotation makes the next rotation faster.

## The Sustainability Factors

Flywheel sustainability depends on specific factors.

+ Quality of execution maintains reputation.
+ Relationship maintenance generates referrals.
+ Process improvement increases efficiency.
+ Team development ensures continuity.

The factors require ongoing investment.

## The Permanent Advantage

Self-sustaining deal flow creates permanent advantage.

* Competitors cannot easily replicate flywheel.
* Advantages compound indefinitely.
* Market changes do not affect flywheel.
* Time reinforces rather than erodes advantage.

The permanent advantage is the ultimate goal.
