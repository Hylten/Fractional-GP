Pipeline management determines conversion success. Proper management transforms signals into closed deals. The firms that manage pipelines effectively convert more opportunities.

## The Pipeline Stages

Stages define progression through process.

> Source: Signals identified and entered.
> Outreach: Initial contact initiated.
> Relationship: Engagement developed.
> Evaluation: Due diligence executed.
> Negotiation: Terms being discussed.
> Closing: Documentation finalized.

The stages connect logically.

## The Stage Metrics

Metrics measure stage performance.

* Volume: Opportunities entering stage.
* Velocity: Time spent in stage.
* Conversion: Percentage advancing.
* Quality: Expected value advancing.

The metrics reveal efficiency.

## The Bottleneck Identification

Bottlenecks limit overall flow.

+ Identify stages with lowest conversion.
+ Analyze causes of low conversion.
+ Prioritize improvements by impact.
+ Implement solutions systematically.

The identification enables optimization.

## The Flow Optimization

Optimization improves overall performance.

* Increase stage conversion rates.
* Reduce stage velocity.
* Balance stage capacities.
* Monitor continuously for issues.

The optimization maximizes output.
