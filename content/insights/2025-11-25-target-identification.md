Target identification generates opportunity. Systematic discovery creates sustainable pipeline. The firms that identify targets effectively never lack opportunities.

## The Identification Criteria

Criteria define acceptable targets.

> Financial thresholds: Revenue and profitability minimums.
> Operational characteristics: Business model and scale.
> Strategic fit: Sector and geographic preferences.
> Owner situation: Motivation indicators.

The criteria narrow universe.

## The Discovery Methods

Methods find targets matching criteria.

* Database searches identify candidates.
* Market analysis reveals sectors.
* Network inquiries find opportunities.
* Direct research uncovers prospects.

The methods generate candidate lists.

## The Qualification Process

Qualification confirms fit.

+ Verify criteria alignment.
+ Assess owner motivation.
+ Evaluate competitive position.
+ Estimate potential value.

The process creates qualified pipeline.

## The Prioritization Framework

Framework ranks opportunities.

* Motivation level scores.
* Strategic fit scores.
* Competitive intensity scores.
* Value potential scores.

The framework guides resource allocation.
