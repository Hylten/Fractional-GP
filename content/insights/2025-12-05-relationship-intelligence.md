Relationship intelligence creates competitive advantage. Systematic knowledge enables better decisions. The firms that build intelligence systematically outperform those that rely on memory.

## The Intelligence Components

Intelligence includes multiple components.

> Contact information: Basic data about relationships.
> Interaction history: Past communications and outcomes.
> Preference data: Communication styles and timing.
* Knowledge notes: Insights about individuals.

The components create comprehensive view.

## The Collection Systems

Collection captures information systematically.

* Every interaction logged.
* Important insights captured.
* Updates tracked over time.
* Context preserved for future use.

The systems build knowledge over time.

## The Analysis Capabilities

Analysis extracts actionable intelligence.

+ Patterns reveal preferences.
+ History predicts behavior.
+ Context informs approach.
+ Knowledge guides decisions.

The capabilities enable better engagement.

## The Integration Benefits

Integration multiplies intelligence value.

* CRM integrates with communication tools.
* Pipeline integrates with CRM.
* Portfolio integrates with pipeline.
* Knowledge integrates across all.

The integration creates comprehensive capability.
