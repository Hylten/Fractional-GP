Structure affects performance. Proper structure enables scale. The firms that structure teams effectively outperform those that organize randomly.

## The Functional Components

Team structure has specific functions.

> Sourcing function: Generates deal flow.
> Evaluation function: Assesses opportunities.
> Execution function: Closes transactions.
> Relationship function: Manages advisor networks.

The functions cover deal flow lifecycle.

## The Specialization Benefits

Specialization improves performance.

* Deep expertise develops in focused areas.
* Efficiency increases through specialization.
* Quality improves with focused attention.
* Velocity accelerates through practice.

The benefits create performance advantages.

## The Coordination Mechanisms

Coordination connects specialized functions.

* Clear handoffs transfer opportunities.
* Shared information enables collaboration.
* Aligned incentives motivate cooperation.
* Unified process ensures consistency.

The mechanisms enable integrated operation.

## The Scalability Design

Scalability requires deliberate design.

+ Modular structure enables addition.
+ Standard processes enable replication.
+ Documentation enables transfer.
+ Culture enables growth.

The design enables sustainable scaling.
