Excellence requires integration. Components alone are insufficient. The firms that integrate effectively achieve deal flow excellence.

## The Integration Vision

Integration vision connects all components.

> Sourcing connects to evaluation.
> Evaluation connects to execution.
> Execution connects to portfolio management.
> Portfolio management connects to relationship building.

The vision creates cohesive operation.

## The System Thinking

System thinking recognizes interconnections.

* Changes in one component affect others.
* Optimization must consider system-wide impact.
* Feedback loops span the entire system.
* Outcomes reflect entire system performance.

The thinking enables holistic optimization.

## The Coordination Mechanisms

Coordination mechanisms enable integration.

* Shared information across functions.
* Aligned incentives across team.
* Unified process connecting stages.
* Clear handoffs between functions.

The mechanisms enable seamless operation.

## The Excellence Standards

Excellence standards demand integration.

+ Quality maintained across all stages.
+ Efficiency achieved in all components.
+ Effectiveness measured at system level.
+ Improvement applied continuously.

The standards enable sustained excellence.
