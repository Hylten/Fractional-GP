Risk affects sustainability. Systematic management enables continuity. The firms that manage risk effectively avoid disruptions.

## The Risk Categories

Deal flow faces multiple risk categories.

> Relationship risks: Advisor departure, relationship deterioration.
> Channel risks: Source loss, competitive pressure.
> Personnel risks: Team turnover, capability gaps.
> Market risks: Sector decline, competitive changes.

The categories require different mitigation.

## The Assessment Process

Assessment identifies and quantifies risks.

* Probability of occurrence.
* Impact if occurs.
* Detectability of early warning.
* Mitigation effectiveness.

The assessment enables prioritization.

## The Mitigation Strategies

Mitigation addresses specific risks.

+ Relationship risks: Portfolio diversification.
+ Channel risks: Multi-channel presence.
+ Personnel risks: Knowledge capture and transfer.
+ Market risks: Sector diversification.

The strategies reduce risk exposure.

## The Monitoring Systems

Monitoring enables early warning.

* Key risk indicators track changes.
* Regular reviews assess risk status.
* Escalation procedures address issues.
* Response plans prepared in advance.

The systems enable rapid response.
