Thesis drives strategy. Coherent thesis enables focus. The firms that operate with clear theses outperform those that pursue opportunities randomly.

## The Thesis Components

Investment thesis has multiple components.

> Sector focus: Where to compete.
> Stage focus: What to acquire.
> Value creation: How to improve.
> Return target: What returns to achieve.

The components create coherent strategy.

## The Development Process

Thesis development follows specific process.

* Market analysis reveals opportunities.
* Capability assessment reveals strengths.
* Resource analysis reveals constraints.
* Strategy integrates findings.

The process creates informed thesis.

## The Execution Alignment

Execution aligns with thesis.

+ Deal criteria match thesis requirements.
+ Sourcing targets thesis sectors.
* Evaluation applies thesis criteria.
* Investment follows thesis parameters.

The alignment ensures coherence.

## The Evolution Framework

Thesis evolves based on learning.

* Market changes update opportunity assessment.
* Capability changes update strength assessment.
* Results update strategy effectiveness.
* Evolution maintains thesis relevance.

The framework keeps thesis current.
