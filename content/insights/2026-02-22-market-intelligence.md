Intelligence supports better decisions. Decision support systems improve outcomes. The firms that build intelligence systematically make better choices.

## The Intelligence Components

Decision intelligence has multiple components.

> Market data: Valuation trends, deal flow volumes, competitive activity.
> Sector data: Industry dynamics, consolidation patterns, growth rates.
> Target data: Business characteristics, ownership situations, timing indicators.
> Process data: Conversion rates, timing patterns, success factors.

The components inform different decisions.

## The Collection Systems

Intelligence collection requires systematic approaches.

* Market monitoring for trends and anomalies.
* Sector analysis for dynamics and opportunities.
* Target identification for acquisition criteria.
* Process tracking for optimization.

The systems generate continuous intelligence.

## The Analysis Frameworks

Analysis transforms data into intelligence.

+ Pattern recognition reveals opportunities.
+ Comparative analysis identifies advantages.
+ Predictive models forecast outcomes.
+ Optimization models recommend actions.

The frameworks enable better decisions.

## The Decision Integration

Intelligence integrates with decision processes.

* Investment committee uses intelligence.
* Sourcing teams use intelligence.
* Deal teams use intelligence.
* Portfolio management uses intelligence.

The integration improves outcomes.
