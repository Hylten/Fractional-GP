Sustainability requires deliberate design. Short-term thinking undermines long-term capability. The firms that design sustainably generate permanent advantages.

## The Sustainability Components

Sustainable deal flow has specific components.

> Repeatable processes that work indefinitely.
> Relationships that deepen over time.
> Systems that improve continuously.
> Culture that survives personnel changes.

The components enable indefinite operation.

## The Design Principles

Design follows specific principles.

* Process documentation ensures continuity.
* Relationship investment builds durable connections.
* Technology enables long-term capability.
* Culture transfers across generations.

The principles create sustainability.

## The Investment Horizon

Sustainable investment requires long horizons.

+ Accept short-term costs for long-term returns.
+ Build capabilities that compound.
+ Maintain relationships across years.
+ Design for decades, not quarters.

The horizons enable sustainability.

## The Permanent Advantage

Sustainability creates permanent advantages.

* Competitors cannot easily replicate.
* Market changes do not affect sustainability.
* Time reinforces rather than erodes advantage.
* The advantage compounds indefinitely.

The advantage is the ultimate goal.
