Successful deals generate more successful deals. The network effects create compounding deal flow. The firms that understand network effects build sustainable advantages.

## The Deal Flow Multipliers

Each successful deal multiplies future deal flow.

> Completed transactions create referenceable sellers.
> Seller satisfaction generates referrals.
> Advisor relationships deepen from positive experiences.
> Market reputation improves from successful outcomes.

The multipliers create exponential growth.

## The Flywheel Effect

Deal flow creates flywheel momentum.

* More deals generate more relationships.
* More relationships generate more deals.
* More deals generate more reputation.
* More reputation generates more deals.

The flywheel accelerates over time.

## The Network Advantage

Network advantages are difficult to replicate.

* Relationships take time to build.
* Reputation takes time to establish.
* Trust takes time to develop.
* Networks take time to mature.

The time investment creates barriers that newcomers cannot cross.

## The Investment Perspective

Network effects require long-term investment.

+ Build relationships before needing them.
+ Accept short-term costs for long-term returns.
* Maintain relationships over extended periods.
* Trust that network will generate returns.

The perspective enables network building.
