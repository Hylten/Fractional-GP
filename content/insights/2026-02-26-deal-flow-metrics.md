Metrics enable optimization. The right metrics reveal opportunities. The firms that measure systematically outperform those that guess.

## The Funnel Metrics

Deal flow funnel metrics track conversion.

> Signal to Contact: Outreach effectiveness.
> Contact to Meeting: Engagement quality.
> Meeting to Offer: Qualification accuracy.
> Offer to Close: Execution capability.

The funnel metrics reveal conversion rates.

## The Velocity Metrics

Velocity metrics track speed.

* Time in Stage: Process efficiency.
* Total Cycle: Deal speed.
* Response Time: Responsiveness.
* Close Time: Execution speed.

The velocity metrics reveal efficiency.

## The Efficiency Metrics

Efficiency metrics track resource utilization.

+ Cost per Deal: Resource efficiency.
+ Cost per Contact: Outreach efficiency.
+ Hours per Deal: Labor efficiency.
+ Team Capacity: Resource planning.

The efficiency metrics reveal optimization opportunities.

## The Quality Metrics

Quality metrics track deal selection.

* Return rates: Investment performance.
* Hold period: Timing accuracy.
* Multiple expansion: Value creation.
* Management changes: Operational improvement.

The quality metrics reveal selection accuracy.

## The Optimization Process

Optimization requires systematic analysis.

+ Compare metrics to benchmarks.
+ Identify underperforming areas.
+ Test changes systematically.
+ Implement successful improvements.
+ Repeat continuously.

The process creates continuous improvement.
