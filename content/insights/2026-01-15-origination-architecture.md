Origination architecture defines how deal flow systems work. Proper architecture enables sustainable generation. The firms that design systems effectively generate deal flow indefinitely.

## The Architecture Components

Architecture has structural components.

> Process layer: How deals move through stages.
> Technology layer: How systems support processes.
> People layer: How teams execute activities.
> Relationship layer: How networks generate opportunities.

The layers connect and interact.

## The Process Design

Process design determines execution efficiency.

* Clear stages define progression.
* Handoffs transfer responsibility.
* Quality gates verify progress.
* Metrics track performance.

The design enables systematic execution.

## The Technology Integration

Technology supports and amplifies processes.

+ Automation reduces manual effort.
+ Integration connects functions.
+ Analytics provide insight.
+ Communication enables coordination.

The integration multiplies capability.

## The People Alignment

People execute processes effectively.

* Clear roles define responsibilities.
* Skills match role requirements.
* Incentives align with objectives.
* Culture supports execution.

The alignment enables consistent execution.

## The Relationship Cultivation

Relationships generate opportunities over time.

* Networks provide access.
* Trust enables conversion.
* Reputation precedes outreach.
* Referrals compound with success.

The cultivation creates sustainable flow.
