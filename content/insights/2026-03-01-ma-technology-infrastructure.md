The system projects outcomes using weighted pipeline value. The analytics enable intervention that improves deal flow. Analytics identified email engagement as primary conversion driver, leading to sequence optimization that improved response rates 28 percent.

Performance metrics track effectiveness across dimensions. The system shows identification rates through source analysis. The system shows engagement effectiveness through channel comparison. The system shows close outcomes through deal-level tracking. The metrics enable evaluation that supports strategic decisions. Metrics revealed mobile outreach outperformed email by 3:1 in manufacturing sector, enabling channel reallocation.
