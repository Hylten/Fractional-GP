Outbound sourcing is active. Inbound deal flow is passive. The best deal flow programs combine both. Building inbound capability creates opportunities that come without active sourcing.

## The Inbound Foundations

Inbound deal flow requires visibility and credibility.

> Market presence makes sellers aware of you as a buyer.
> Credibility makes sellers consider you as a buyer.
> Differentiation makes you stand out from competitors.
> Accessibility makes it easy to reach you.

The foundations create the conditions for inbound inquiry.

## The Content Strategy

Content builds market presence and credibility.

* Educational content demonstrates expertise.
* Transaction content shows capability.
* Thought leadership establishes authority.
* Community presence builds reputation.

The content strategy targets seller audiences. The content addresses seller concerns. The content demonstrates buyer readiness.

+ Publish regularly on relevant platforms.
+ Create content that addresses seller pain points.
+ Distribute through channels that sellers use.
+ Measure inbound response to optimize approach.

## The Inbound Channels

Multiple channels generate inbound interest.

> Website optimization captures search traffic.
> Professional networks build visibility.
> Industry events create presence.
> Advisor relationships generate referrals.

The channel mix varies by target sector. The optimized mix maximizes inbound volume.

## The Conversion System

Inbound leads require systematic conversion.

* Respond quickly to inquiries.
* Qualify fit efficiently.
* Build relationship rapidly.
* Convert interest to process.

The conversion system captures inbound value. Without conversion capability, inbound volume is wasted.
