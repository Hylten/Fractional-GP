Deal flow comes from multiple channels. Channel mix affects cost, quality, and sustainability. The firms that optimize channel mix outperform those that rely on single sources.

## The Channel Types

Deal flow channels have distinct characteristics.

> Direct outreach: High control, variable cost, moderate conversion.
> Advisor relationships: High quality, relationship dependent, limited scalability.
> Inbound marketing: Low cost, high volume, variable quality.
> Platform acquisition: High quality, high cost, strategic value.

The types vary by cost and quality.

## The Mix Optimization

Channel mix optimization considers multiple factors.

* Cost per deal by channel.
* Quality of deals by channel.
* Sustainability of channel access.
* Strategic value of channel relationships.

The optimization balances factors.

## The Diversification Benefits

Diversification provides multiple benefits.

+ Reduces channel risk.
+ Improves negotiation position.
+ Creates optionality.
+ Enables market expansion.

The benefits outweigh coordination costs.

## The Integration Approach

Channel integration creates compound effects.

* Referrals from one channel feed other channels.
* Relationships span multiple channels.
* Intelligence crosses channel boundaries.
* Optimization requires holistic view.

The approach maximizes channel value.
