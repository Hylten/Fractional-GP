Deal flow culture extends beyond the sourcing team. The right culture generates opportunities throughout the organization. The firms that build culture capture deals that competitors miss.

## The Culture Components

Deal flow culture has organizational components.

> Leadership sets priorities and expectations.
> Employees generate referrals and contacts.
> Portfolio companies create opportunities.
> Advisors spread reputation.
> Investors provide network access.

The components span the organization.

## The Alignment Mechanisms

Alignment mechanisms connect deal flow to incentives.

* Referral bonuses reward employees.
* Network development credits advisors.
* Relationship development supports portfolio companies.
* Investor relations generate introductions.

The mechanisms motivate deal flow activity.

## The Capture Systems

Capture systems ensure opportunities are captured.

* Referral processes streamline submissions.
* Pipeline tracking monitors progress.
* Follow-up systems ensure conversion.
* Credit systems attribute correctly.

The systems prevent lost opportunities.

## The Cultural Development

Culture development requires sustained effort.

+ Model behavior from leadership.
+ Recognize deal flow contributions.
+ Celebrate successful captures.
+ Continuously improve systems.

The development creates lasting capability.
