Origination capability creates sustainable deal generation. The right capabilities generate deals without constant effort. The firms that build capabilities generate deal flow indefinitely.

## The Capability Dimensions

Capability has multiple dimensions.

> People: Team with sourcing skills.
> Process: System for executing activities.
> Technology: Tools supporting execution.
> Relationships: Networks generating opportunities.

The dimensions create complete capability.

## The Development Pathway

Development follows specific pathway.

* Assess current capabilities.
* Identify gaps between current and desired.
* Design development plan.
* Execute development activities.
* Measure capability improvement.

The pathway creates systematic growth.

## The Investment Requirements

Investment enables capability development.

+ Time for building skills.
+ Capital for tools and systems.
+ Patience for relationship development.
+ Commitment for long-term focus.

The investment generates returns.

## The Compounding Returns

Compounding amplifies capability value.

* Better capabilities attract talent.
* Talent improves capabilities.
* Improved capabilities generate deals.
* Deals provide resources for more investment.

The compounding creates upward spiral.
