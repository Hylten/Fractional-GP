## The Reactive Pursuit Problem

Traditional acquisition pursues opportunities that surface. Broker listings announce targets. Industry contacts share rumors. Market events generate announcements. The approach waits for opportunities to appear. The waiting creates competition that inflates prices.

Signal detection systems transform pursuit from reactive to proactive. The systems identify opportunities before announcements. The identification creates access that reactive approaches cannot match. The access improves returns that competition eliminates.

Average competitive processes reduced to 2.1 bidders.
