Market timing affects deal flow quality. Understanding timing patterns enables better capital deployment. The firms that time markets generate better returns.

## The Market Cycles

Markets cycle between expansion and contraction.

* Expansion: Valuations rise. Deal flow increases. Competition intensifies.
* Peak: Valuations peak. Deal flow peaks. Competition peaks.
* Contraction: Valuations fall. Deal flow decreases. Competition decreases.
* Trough: Valuations bottom. Deal flow bottoms. Competition minimal.

The cycle creates timing opportunities.

## The Deal Flow Patterns

Deal flow follows predictable patterns.

> Expansion generates more deals but at higher prices.
> Contraction generates fewer deals but at better prices.
> Timing affects both volume and pricing.

The patterns inform deployment strategy.

## The Deployment Strategy

Capital deployment should align with timing.

* Expansion: Deploy capital selectively at high prices or wait.
* Contraction: Deploy aggressively at low prices.
* Build relationships during contraction.
* Execute during timing opportunities.

The strategy requires patience and discipline.

## The Return Impact

Timing affects returns directly.

+ Lower purchase prices enable higher returns.
+ Higher purchase prices compress returns.
+ Timing advantage compounds across portfolio.
+ Consistent timing generates alpha.
