Investment allocation determines deal flow returns. Proper allocation maximizes output. The firms that allocate effectively generate superior returns on sourcing investments.

## The Investment Categories

Investment spans multiple categories.

> Personnel: Sourcing team salaries and incentives.
> Technology: Tools and infrastructure.
> Marketing: Events and content.
> Relationships: Advisor and network development.

The categories require different allocations.

## The Return on Investment

ROI varies by category.

* Personnel ROI depends on productivity.
* Technology ROI depends on efficiency gains.
* Marketing ROI depends on lead generation.
* Relationship ROI depends on deal flow.

The ROI guides allocation decisions.

## The Optimization Strategy

Strategy allocates for maximum return.

+ Increase investment in high-ROI categories.
+ Reduce investment in low-ROI categories.
+ Test new categories for expansion.
* Maintain balanced portfolio.

The strategy maximizes returns.

## The Rebalancing Approach

Approach adjusts over time.

* Monitor ROI continuously.
* Adjust allocations based on results.
* Test new approaches for improvement.
* Maintain flexibility for changes.

The approach keeps investments optimized.
