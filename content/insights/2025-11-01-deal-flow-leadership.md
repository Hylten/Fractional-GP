Leadership drives deal flow capability. Effective leadership creates sustainable generation. The firms with strong leadership generate deal flow indefinitely.

## The Leadership Components

Leadership has multiple components.

> Vision: Understanding of opportunity landscape.
> Strategy: Approach to capturing opportunities.
* Culture: Values that guide behavior.
> Systems: Infrastructure that enables execution.

The components create capability.

## The Vision Development

Vision comes from market understanding.

* Deep sector expertise reveals opportunities.
* Relationship experience shows patterns.
* Technology awareness enables innovation.
* Competition analysis informs strategy.

The vision guides decisions.

## The Culture Cultivation

Culture enables execution.

+ Accountability for results.
+ Collaboration across functions.
+ Innovation in approaches.
+ Persistence through challenges.

The culture drives performance.

## The System Building

Systems enable scale.

* Processes document best practices.
* Technology amplifies capability.
* Metrics enable optimization.
* Training develops skills.

The systems enable growth.
