Valuation precision determines returns. Proprietary deal flow enables precise valuation. Auction dynamics force valuation assumptions that destroy returns.

## The Information Advantage

Proprietary deals provide information advantages.

> Direct owner contact enables nuanced understanding.
> Due diligence depth reveals true value.
> Relationship context informs risk assessment.
> Time allows thorough analysis.

The information advantage produces precise valuation. Precise valuation enables appropriate pricing.

## The Timing Control

Proprietary timing provides control that auctions lack.

* No competitive pressure to overpay.
* No time pressure to skip analysis.
* No pressure to make assumptions.
* Complete information enables precise decision.

The timing control eliminates the errors that auctions force.

## The Pricing Discipline

Proprietary deals require pricing discipline.

* Value accurately based on information.
* Price appropriately based on value.
* Decline deals that do not meet return thresholds.
* Wait for right opportunities.

The pricing discipline preserves returns. The discipline is easier to maintain without auction pressure.

## The Return Impact

Pricing precision affects returns directly.

+ Accurate valuation enables appropriate pricing.
+ Appropriate pricing preserves margin.
+ Margin preservation enables returns.
+ Returns compound with scale.

The auction dynamic forces pricing assumptions. Assumptions create errors. Errors destroy returns.
