Internal sources include CRM records through Salesforce, deal files through document management, and team knowledge through knowledge bases. External sources include market databases through PitchBook, news feeds through Bloomberg, and professional networks through LinkedIn. Collection scope determines intelligence breadth. Collection achieved 94 percent coverage of target universe.

Enrichment adds context from supplementary sources. Processing quality determines analytical accuracy. Processing achieved 96 percent data quality.

Storage systems maintain historical intelligence for pattern recognition. Time-series data enables trend analysis using temporal databases. Relational data enables relationship mapping using graph databases. The storage architecture determines analytical capability. Storage maintained 10 years of historical data.

Distribution systems deliver intelligence to decision-makers. Alert systems notify of significant developments through push notifications. Dashboard systems provide ongoing visibility through web portals. Request systems respond to specific questions through search interfaces. Distribution effectiveness determines intelligence value realization. Distribution achieved 100 percent decision-maker coverage.
