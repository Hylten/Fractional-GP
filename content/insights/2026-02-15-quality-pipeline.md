Quality determines outcomes. Pipeline quality affects deal quality. The firms that maintain quality systematically outperform those that accept variability.

## The Quality Standards

Standards define acceptable performance.

> Minimum criteria for target selection.
> Required information for evaluation.
> Necessary capabilities for closing.
> Expected performance post-acquisition.

The standards span the pipeline.

## The Quality Checks

Checks verify standards at each stage.

* Stage-gates prevent progression without quality.
* Reviews assess quality at transitions.
* Audits verify ongoing compliance.
* Feedback improves standards over time.

The checks maintain quality throughout.

## The Quality Investment

Quality requires investment.

+ Time for thorough evaluation.
+ Resources for comprehensive analysis.
+ Patience for proper process.
+ Discipline for consistent standards.

The investment generates returns.

## The Quality Culture

Quality requires cultural commitment.

* Leadership models quality behavior.
* Recognition rewards quality performance.
* Accountability enforces quality standards.
* Improvement continuously raises quality.

The culture ensures long-term quality.
